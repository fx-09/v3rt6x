# Code of Conduct for this Repository

If I verify a reported Code of Conduct violation, my policy is:

- To foster an open and welcoming environment, I (fx-09) as contributor and maintainer pledge to make participation in my projects a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

- Examples of behaviour that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

  - Using welcoming and inclusive language.

  - Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.

  - Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.

  - Focusing on what is best for the community.

  - Showing empathy towards other community members.

  - The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.

  - Public or private harassment.
